Sum of Longs

Write a program that takes two large numbers as input, converts them to Long, and prints the result of their summation.

Input: two long numbers as strings (each starting on new line).

Output: Long value.


Sample Input:
1000000000000000
2222222222222222

Sample Output:
3222222222222222



메모리 제한: 256MB
시간 제한: 8 초
